3. Permissions Used

Flashify requires the following permissions for its core functionality:

  • Camera: Used only to toggle the flashlight. No photos or videos are taken.
  • Vibration: Provides haptic feedback when toggling the flashlight or using SOS mode.
  • Foreground Service: Keeps the flashlight active while the app is in background.
  • Post Notifications: Shows a notification when the flashlight is active (Android 13+).
  • Internet: Required for displaying Google AdMob ads.
  • Access Network State: Ensures ads are only requested when connected to the internet.

4. Third-Party Services
